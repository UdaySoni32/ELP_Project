Our Goal

We are dedicated to raising awareness about the critical issue of biodiversity loss and its connection to our food systems. Our mission is to provide clear, accessible information about how alternative proteins—like lab-grown and plant-based meats—can be a powerful tool in the fight to protect our planet's precious ecosystems.

This project is a part of the ELP (Experiential Learning Program) at Woxsen University. The members of our group are: Uday Soni, Dipen Kedia, Yashwin Gupta, and Deep.

By showcasing the benefits of these innovative foods and connecting you with the companies that produce them, we hope to inspire a movement towards a more sustainable and compassionate way of eating.
